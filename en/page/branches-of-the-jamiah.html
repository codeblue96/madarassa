Branches of Jamiah

In addition to the main center (boys and girls departments) of Jamiah (est. 1954), there are fourteen branches of Jamiah, in which approximately 12,028 students(boys and girls)are studying according to the year 1445 AH (2023), the number of teachers (male and female) in these branches is 423, while the number of other staff (male and female) is 238.The list of all branches is as follows:

  1. Madrasah Taleem ul Islam, Gulshan e Umar, Sohrab Goth, Karachi, est. 1975
  2. Madrasah Arabiyyah Islamiyyah, Ghazi Town, Maleer, Karachi, est. 1985 
  3. Madrasah Arabiyyah Islamiyyah, Chilia, Thatta, Sindh, est. 1988 
  4. Madrasah Khulafa e Rashideen, Old Golimar, Karachi, est. 1990
  5. Madrasah Rahmaniyyah, Bilal Colony, Mills Area, Karachi, est. 1991     
  6. Madrasah Rasheediyyah, PIB Colony, Karachi, est. 1996 
  7. Madrasah Hafsah (for Girls), Shah Faisal Colony, Karachi, est. 1996
  8. Madrasah Badr ul Uloom, Shah Nawaz Bhutto Colony, Karachi, est. 1997 
  9. Madrasah Taleem ul Quraan, Wali Muhammad Goth, Maleer, Karachi, est. 1997     
  10. Madrasah Dar ul Uloom Usmaniyyah, Bihar Colony, Karachi, est. 1999     
  11. Madrasah Taleem ul Islam, near Islamiyyah College, Karachi, est. 2000 
  12. Madrasah Shams ul Uloom, Sultanabad, New Haji Camp, Karachi, est. 2015
  13. Deeni Madrasah Taleem ul Quraan, Bihar Colony, Karachi, est. 2017
  14. Madrasah Aaminah (for Girls), Quetta Town, Karachi, est. 2018
